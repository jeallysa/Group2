Gender Equality

	Gender Equality is about the fulfilment of equal rights for men and women,
Women differ from men both in terms of their biological capacities and in the socially constructed disadvantages
they currently face. Inequalities comes from unequal power relation between men and women, 
and therefore the progress towards gender equality in every country are needed to establish whether the changes
that are being achieved can make changes to these relations. The removing of the social norms
that see men and women as making unequal contributions to the society and having unequal right to 
its benefits is hard to achieve in a society free from gender discrimination. Thus whether men and women are being treated equally, 
it will still depend on the freedom and choice they face are still the same
